My Uber Rides

Duration

2 Weeks

Categories

Data Visualisation

A classroom project wherein a data visualisation had to created representing an aspect of our own lives, with the aim of discovering new insights and communicating our findings clearly to the viewers. This visualisation explores the data of all my Uber rides since I started using the app in 2016, aiming to find patterns in how I travel.
